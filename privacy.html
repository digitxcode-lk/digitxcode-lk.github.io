Privacy Policy

At DigitXCode, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to ensure better communication and service quality:

  • Name: To identify you and personalize our services
  • Phone Number: For direct communication and appointment confirmations
  • Email Address: For sending important updates, meeting summaries, and payment links
  • Project Details: Information you provide about your projects and requirements
How We Use Your Information
  • To provide and improve our consultancy services
  • To communicate with you about appointments and services
  • To send meeting summaries and project documentation
  • To provide ongoing WhatsApp support
  • To ensure service quality and professional standards
Data Retention Policy
  • Meeting Recordings: All records of meeting video recordings are kept until 6 months after service term ends
  • WhatsApp Support Chats: Support conversations are retained for 6 months after service completion
  • Personal Information: All client's personally identifiable information is deleted after 1 year of no service activity
  • Payment Records: Financial transaction records are kept as per legal requirements
What We Don't Collect

We prioritize your security and never ask for:

  • Credit or debit card details through any communication channel
  • Bank account usernames or passwords
  • Social media account credentials
  • Any sensitive financial information via email, WhatsApp, or meetings
  • Personal documents or identity verification unless legally required
Data Security Measures
  • All personal data is stored on secure, encrypted servers
  • Meeting recordings are stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel
  • Payment processing is handled through secure third-party providers
  • Regular security audits and updates to protect your information
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data we hold
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • File a complaint about our data handling practices
Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Payment Processing: Secure payment gateways for transaction processing
  • Communication: WhatsApp Business for customer support
  • Video Meetings: Secure video conferencing platforms
  • Email Services: Professional email providers for communication

All third-party services are carefully selected and comply with data protection standards.

Changes to Privacy Policy
  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time
  • Significant changes will be communicated via email
  • Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of updated terms
  • Previous versions are archived and available upon request
